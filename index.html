Oreo Cake Recipe

Ingridients

    Choclate Cake

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot coffee,you can subtitute boiling water

    • Oreo Froasting

  • 1 1/2 cups unsalted butter
  • 6 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 cup oreo crumbs,finely processed
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees and butter and dust with cocoa powder two 9 inch cake pans.
  2. In a stand mixer on low speed mix together the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda and salt
  3. In a small bowl whisk together the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla then add
    it to the stand mixer on low then medium speed until just combined and finally add in the water carefully and
    stir it in by hand until just combined (do not turn on the mixer, it may splash on you).
  4. Pour half of the batter into each of the pans and bake for 30-35 minutes
    then remove, let cool completely and frost with the oreo frosting.
  5. To make the frosting add the butter to your stand mixer on medium speed for 30 seconds
    until light and fluffy then add in the powdered sugar and milk in alternating amounts.
  6. Add in the oreo crumbs and vanilla last until just combined.
  7. To frost cleanly without crumbs showing through add frosting to the middle of the cake as normal,
    a thin layer to the outside of the cake and refrigerate 2 hours before frosting a second finished layer
